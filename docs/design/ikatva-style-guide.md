# IKATVA Brand Style Guide

This document defines the visual identity for the IKATVA e-commerce platform, focusing on heritage, craftsmanship, and luxury.

---

## Brand Vibe & Vision
- **Earthy & Elegant:** Grounded in natural materials and sophisticated design.
- **Cultural & Premium:** Celebrating Indian traditions with a high-end, boutique feel.
- **Museum-Quality:** The site should feel like a curated gallery of artful crafts.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette

### 1. Heritage Ivory (Primary Background)
**HEX:** `#F5EFE6`
- **Use for:** Website background, product pages, main sections.
- **Why:** Feels like handmade paper; provides an elegant, warm canvas that lets products stand out.

### 2. Deep Maroon (Primary Brand Color)
**HEX:** `#6B1F2B`
- **Use for:** Buttons, headings, navbar highlights, primary call-to-actions.
- **Why:** Strongly associated with Indian royalty and traditional textiles.

### 3. Antique Gold (Luxury Accent)
**HEX:** `#C8A96A`
- **Use for:** Icons, borders, hover effects, decorative lines.
- **Why:** Communicates luxury, heritage, and the fine details of craftsmanship.

### 4. Sandstone Beige (Secondary Background)
**HEX:** `#E6D5B8`
- **Use for:** Alternate sections, cards, background textures.
- **Why:** Adds a natural, earthy craft vibe that balances the luxury elements.

### 5. Charcoal Black (Text Color)
**HEX:** `#2B2B2B`
- **Use for:** Body text, product names, UI labels.
- **Why:** Softer than pure black; maintains a premium, organic feel.

---

## 📐 Design Rules

### The 80-15-5 Rule
To maintain a premium and elegant look, follow this distribution:
- **80% Neutrals:** (Heritage Ivory / Sandstone Beige) for the overall layout.
- **15% Brand Color:** (Deep Maroon) for primary elements and emphasis.
- **5% Accent Color:** (Antique Gold) for subtle luxury sparks.

### Homepage Usage Examples
- **Navbar:** Ivory background with Charcoal text. Maroon for hover states.
- **Hero Section:** High-res image with Ivory overlay text. Maroon CTA button with Gold hover effect.
- **Product Cards:** Ivory background, Charcoal titles, and Maroon pricing.

---

## ✨ Visual Brand Feel
IKATVA should evoke the feeling of:
- **Indian Textiles** (Silk, Khadi, Handloom)
- **Handmade Craft** (Emroidery, Ikat, Woodwork)
- **Heritage Museum** (Curated, Historic, Story-rich)
- **Luxury Boutique** (Exclusive, High-quality, Personal)

---

## 🖋️ Typography

### 1. The Heritage Header (Primary Serif)
**Font:** [Cormorant Garamond](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cormorant+Garamond)
- **Vibe:** Sharp, regal, and historic.
- **Use for:** Main headlines, product names, and "Heritage" section titles.

### 2. The Modern Guide (Primary Sans-Serif)
**Font:** [Outfit](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Outfit)
- **Vibe:** Clean, geometric, and perfectly readable.
- **Use for:** Navigation, buttons, prices, and long body text.

### 3. The Artisan’s Voice (Decorative Script)
**Font:** [Great Vibes](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Great+Vibes)
- **Vibe:** Warm, personal, and handwritten.
- **Use for:** Small artisan quotes, "Handmade" annotations, and decorative accents.
