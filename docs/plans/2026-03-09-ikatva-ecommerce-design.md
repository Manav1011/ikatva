# IKATVA E-commerce Site Design

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Status:** In Progress
**Project:** Transitioning IKATVA from info-site to e-commerce platform.

## Overview
IKATVA is a luxury heritage brand rooted in unity through tradition and strength through culture. The e-commerce site aims to sell handmade, heritage-inspired products (purses, etc.) while empowering Indian women artisans and preserving traditional crafts.

## Target Audience
- People in India who love art and traditional craftsmanship.
- Supporters of women's empowerment.
- Customers seeking ethically made, high-quality heritage luxury.
